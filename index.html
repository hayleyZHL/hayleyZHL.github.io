Projects

Bank Failures

Following the recent shutdown of Silicon Valley Bank, I began to ponder whether this is a sign of another recession, or if we are already in one. To gain some perspective, I took a look at similar bank failures that have occurred in the past few decades. As a result, I have put together a visualization of bank failure cases since 2000.

Business Finder

If you're looking to explore the businesses of a particular area, what's the best way to do so using visualization? One effective approach is to apply the context + focus design principle. This allows users to obtain an overview of all the businesses in the area while also being able to access specific details about a business they may be interested in.

Crime v.s. Business

Have you ever wondered how crime rates impact businesses? In this visualization, we take a closer look at the distribution of crime and businesses in Philadelphia. As expected, we found that higher crime rates tend to correlate with fewer businesses. However, we also discovered one district that has high numbers of both businesses and crimes.

Business after Pandemic?

During the recent pandemic, many were wondering how it has affected the business landscape and whether it's a good time to start a new venture. To shed some light on this topic, we've visualized the reviews of businesses before and after the pandemic. It appears that there has been a significant drop in the number of reviews for businesses on Yelp.

What is your Pokémon?

Welcome to the fascinating world of Pokémon, where there's a Pokémon for everyone to enjoy. Our visualization showcases the wealth of information available on these extraordinary beings. Come check it out and collect your favorite Pokémons today!

Have we planted enough trees?

Our climate is changing dramatically, largely due to the extra greenhouse gases emitted by human activities. Planting more trees can help mitigate these effects, and not to mention, trees are beautiful! Curious about San Francisco's tree planting efforts? Our visualization offers insights into the popular types of trees, the number of trees planted, and more.
